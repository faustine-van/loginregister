About us

Murakaza neza ku bufasha rusange bwatangiye muri 2019

Ubufasha Rusange bwatangijwe n'urubyiruko, babazwaga cyane no kubona abana bakiri bato bazira ibyo ababyeyi babo bakoze akaba ari bo bigiraho ingaruka kandi ataribo babihisemo, harimo ngo (GUTANDUKANA KWA BABYEYI,ABANA BIRERA,ABABYEYI BATA ABANA, ABANA BAFITE ABABYEYI BARI MURI GEREZA,...) ibi bikaba ari imbogamizi zo kugira icyo bigezaho cyangwa kuba haricyo bakora kuribo kandi aribo Rwanda rwejo. Bityo tukaba ntaho twaba tugana tutita kubo twita u Rwanda rwejo kugira ngo nabo bagirire i gihugu cyacu akamaro kandi twimakaza indangagaciro nyarwanda harimo no gufashanya.

Tukaba twariyemeje gufasha abo bana kugirango bagire uburenganzira nku bwabandi bana kugirango bazagire ejo hazaza heza kandi i gihugu cyizeye ko gifite amaboko azagikorera ni muri urwo rwego dusaba abantu bafite umutima utabara cyangwa umutima ufasha ko buri wese yakitanga ko ashoboye yaba inkunga ya Masengesho,imyambaro,ibyo kurya,amafaranga,nubundi bufasha butandukanye mwagenera abo bana. Tukaba tubizeza ko aba bana muzafasha batazabatenguha bazavamo abasore ni nkumi nziza zibereye u Rwanda na Banyarwanda.
